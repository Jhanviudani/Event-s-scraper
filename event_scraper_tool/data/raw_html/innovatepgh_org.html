Bio Breakfast

  • Tue, Jul 8, 2025 8:00 AM 08:00 Tue, Dec 16, 2025 10:00 AM 10:00
  • Google Calendar ICS

Pittsburgh’s Biomedical Sector has long been very siloed. The medical professionals and university researchers do not interact with private industry enough and vice versa. Big companies and small companies are often completely disconnected. 

The BioBreakfast is an attempt to bridge the divides between the disparate groups that shape the regional biomedical sector. Most importantly, the Breakfasts try to spur informal networking so that people can build stronger networks and help identify opportunities and individuals they can work with to advance ideas.

The BioBreakfasts happen every Tuesday unless that Tuesday is a National Holiday.  Otherwise, they are on.  You do not need to register. There is no cost. Just show up and take part.

​Rene Canady of Equity Systems Design Consulting

​Equity Systems Design Consulting bridges the gap between biomedical engineering and real-world social needs through strategy, inclusive design, research, and education.

​Georgia Crowther of Reclamation Factory

​Reclamation Factory is making plastic recycling real and accessible by building automated recycling micro-factories that turn plastic waste into new, useful products on-site.

​Sophia Fang of Peachy Day

​Peachy Day is an integrative migraine care app providing health tracking, neurologist video visits, and coaching to reduce migraine severity for life.

​Mitchell Fogelson of Beyond Reach Labs

​Beyond Reach Labs is creating rapidly deployable infrastructure for defense and disaster relief.

​Victoria Kallsen of Build Bedrock

​Build Bedrock is a revenue generation agency that lands tech startups their first 50 B2B customers.

​Miles Mufuka Martin of Relai

​Relai is building hyperlocal infrastructure so anyone can drop off and pick up items asynchronously.

​Priscilla Prem of Pittsburgh Coastal Energy

​Pittsburgh Coastal Energy is powering underwater drones using energy harvested from ocean waves.

​Olivia Suchko of Olive

​Olive is democratizing made-to-measure clothing by modernizing the product development process with technology and robotics.

​Prem Viswanathan of SwiftCX

​SwiftCX is an AI-enabled CX platform that increases customer satisfaction through automation and helps CX teams be more efficient.
